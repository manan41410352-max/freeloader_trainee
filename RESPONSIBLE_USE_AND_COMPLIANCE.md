# Responsible Use and Compliance Policy

Freeloader is intended only for users who:

- Own the prompts, files, and data they submit, or are fully authorized to process them.
- Intend to create structured comparison data for personal research, evaluation, or training on data they control.

The following uses are prohibited by this repository policy and are not endorsed or supported:

- Bypassing authentication, rate limits, paywalls, captchas, or technical protections.
- Automated bulk extraction, scraping, or harvesting of third-party data or outputs.
- Collecting, storing, publishing, or redistributing content you do not have the legal right to possess or share.
- Using the Project in a way that violates the terms of service, acceptable use policy, or other agreement of any third-party service, including ChatGPT, OpenAI services, model providers, browser vendors, or hosting platforms.

You are solely responsible for:

- Verifying that your use of the ChatGPT browser-automation workflow complies with the current OpenAI terms and policies that apply to your account and use case.
- Verifying that your Ollama usage, and every local model you run through Ollama, complies with its own license and restrictions.
- Meeting all legal, regulatory, privacy, copyright, confidentiality, and contractual obligations related to prompts, attachments, browser sessions, stored responses, and exported datasets.

The maintainer does not monitor your compliance, does not approve your use case, and does not accept responsibility for your legal or policy decisions.

If you are unsure whether your intended use is allowed, do not use the Project.

The Project provides no workaround, exemption, or legal cover for violating the rules of any third-party platform.
